PRECAUTIONS

The safety rules everyone should know

  • Regular inspections of cylinders

    Ensure all gas cylinders are thoroughly inspected for leaks, damage, and expiry before dispatch. Use leak detection methods like soapy water or gas detectors to prevent accidents during storage or delivery.

  • Safe storage condition

    Store cylinders upright in a well-ventilated area away from direct sunlight, flammable materials, or electrical equipment. The storage area should have proper fire extinguishers and be clearly marked as a high-risk zone.

  • Turn off when not in use

    Always turn off the regulator and appliance knob after cooking.Place the LPG cylinder on a flat, ventilated surface in an upright position. Never keep it lying down or in a closed space like cabinets or near drains. This prevents accidental leaks and enhances safety.
